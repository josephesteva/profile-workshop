Skiing

Skiing is an awesome sport that is tons of fun! North America has some great mountains for skiing. It is worth it to travel out West, where the skiing is significantly better than on the East Coast! The views are absolutely beautiful and the terrain is tons of fun!

As a new skier, it is best to stick to green routes and stay on groomed trails. Intermediate skiers can comfortably ski on more varied terrain and begin to take advantage of the really fun stuff. Skiing through tree runs and moguls is incredibly fun and rewarding for skiers who are up to the challenge!

Snowboarding is also fun but skiing is better!
